Debate Summarization Evaluation

This survey is part of an evaluation for a computer science research project. We are investigating how to automatically summarise social media debates and are trying to evaluate how readers perceive different automatically generated summaries.

Please contact c.egan.12@aberdeen.ac.uk should you have any queries before starting the survey.
